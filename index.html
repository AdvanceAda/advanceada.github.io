Delegate Now

1. Choose Your Wallet

Start by selecting a Cardano-compatible wallet, such as Daedalus, Yoroi, or Eternl. Make sure your ADA is safely stored and your wallet is fully synced with the network before proceeding.

Delegate Now

2. Find ADVAN Stake Pool

Within your wallet’s delegation interface, search for our pool by its ticker: ADVAN or pool ID. Review the pool details if you like, then select it as your delegation target.

Delegate Now

3. Delegate Your ADA

Decide how much ADA you want to delegate and confirm the transaction. Your delegation will be active after the next epoch, and you’ll start earning rewards while supporting the ADVAN stake pool.
